About Geneva's Trading Post

Alaskan fiber art blending mathematical concepts with traditional craftsmanship. Each piece is handcrafted using natural fibers, home-tanned furs, and meticulous embroidery.

Featured at Southeast Alaska fairs and winner of Best in Show for knitting at the Southeast Alaska State Fair.
